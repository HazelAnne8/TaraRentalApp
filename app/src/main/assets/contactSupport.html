We are here for you always.

Please contact us if there is anything wrong with the car or the host. We are always available to assist you with rebooking, refunds, and any such issues.

When the TARA app isn't working properly, try the following:

  • (1) Make sure you have the most recent version of the program.
    (2) Verify that your internet connection is operational.
    (3) Try restarting or reinstalling the app.
    (4) Login out of your account and then sign back in.
    (5) Verify that location permission is granted.

    • If the issue persists, please take a snapshot of the error message, or record your screen and submit it to tara.vehiclerentalapp2022@gmail.com.
    We may need additional information from you to assist you further.
